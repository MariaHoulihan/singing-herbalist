About the Singing Herbalist


Jane Doe is the singing herbalist, she lives with her family in East Clare.
Jane graduated from NUI, Galway many moons ago with an honours degree in Botany.
Jane studied herbalism at Brigid's Academy for Healing Arts and is a practising herbalist for over 10 years.
