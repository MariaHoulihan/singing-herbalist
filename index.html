The Singing Herbalist's favourite hot drink



The singing herbalist's loves this hot drink because it is so good for the digestion.
Apple cider vinegar is rich in gut friendly bacteria that help the digestive system to work properly.
Ginger too is helpful for the digestion especially for nausea. Ginger has anti-inflammatory and pain-relieving properties. Ginger is warming and improves blood flow.


Apple Cider Vinegar & Ginger Tea


  • 2 tbsp raw apple cider vinegar
  • a few slices of peeled ginger root
  • 1/4 tsp of ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 lemon (juiced)
  • pinch of cayenne pepper
  • 500ml of cold water
  • honey to taste (optional)

  • Put the water, ginger slices and cinnamon into a saucepan.
  • Place over a medium heat. Once it comes to the boil, reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  • Carefully remove the saucepan from the heat, add the apple cider vinegar, lemon juice, cayenne pepper and honey (if using) to the saucepan. Stir.
  • Carefully strain and pour into a heatproof cup/mug. Allow to cool and drink whilst still warm. Serves one.
